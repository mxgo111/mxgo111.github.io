About Me

Max is currently a third-year undergraduate student at Harvard University studying Computer Science and Statistics. His academic interests include machine learning and artificial intelligence, especially with a focus on societal applications. He hopes to pursue a doctorate degree in AI for Social Good in the near future.

Max's experiences include research in machine learning, teaching, quantitative finance, and educational programming. These involve, respectively, collaborations with the CRISP Group and the LIT lab at Harvard, assistant teaching for several Harvard courses, a trading internship with Five Rings Capital LLC, and conference directing and managing finances for the Harvard Association for U.S. China Relations.

Max also spends time pondering faith, practicing calisthenics, playing the piano, and juggling.
